One of the most popular tourist destination in Costa Rica, the Central Pacific region of the country is also the most easily accessible. Just a short distance from the capital San Jose either by domestic airplane or by car, this area is known for its beautiful beaches, touristy coastal resort towns and fantastic surfing opportunities.

Banana Go

Is a funny adventure for 10-15 person in the banana. Get ready for thrills as you bounce and jump along the Pacific on one of our banana boats. This is great economical fun for the whole family!

Kayaking adventure

A kayak is a small, narrow watercraft which is propelled by means of a double-bladed paddle. The traditional kayak has a covered deck and one or more cockpits, each seating one paddler.

Snorkeling

Snorkeling is the practice of swimming on or through a body of water while equipped with a diving mask, a shaped breathing tube called a snorkel, and usually swimfins.
